Project Name: "Lug-A-Jug" Brochure
Client: United Solar Initiative
Timeframe: October 2016



United Solar Initiative | "Lug-A-Jug" Brochure

I had the opportunity to design both the logo and marketing material for United Solar's Lug-A-Jug event that occurred on October 15, 2016. The event aimed to raise awareness toward the everyday struggle some have to obtain drinking water, while also raising money to install solar-powered drinking wells to provide dependable access to water for over 80,000 people. The event raised over $2,000 for its cause.
